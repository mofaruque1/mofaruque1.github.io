MADINA GRILL

With its rustic interior, pops of bright color and welcoming atmosphere, Madina Grill is the best indian restaurant in the heart of Scarborough that encapsulates the vibrant culinary culture of South Asia. Visitors will feel as though they have strolled right off the streets of India and into an authentic neighborhood place.
